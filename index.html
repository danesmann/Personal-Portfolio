02 Born In Red Photography, Story Telling 2025
8:3 hero
Logo

Directed the visual narrative for Born In Red, a photography-driven project exploring cultural identity through everyday rituals. My role centered on art direction and editorial sequencing — curating images into a cohesive arc that felt intimate without becoming sentimental. The critical insight was letting silence do the work: restraining captions, letting compositions breathe, and trusting viewers to find their own meaning. Each image was selected not for individual impact but for how it converses with its neighbors, creating rhythm and emotional pacing across the full series.

08 Mandala Hotel Branding, Illustration 2023
8:3 hero
Logo

Created the brand identity and bespoke illustration suite for Mandala Hotel, a boutique property drawing on Southeast Asian heritage. My role bridged strategic positioning and hands-on illustration — developing a visual language honoring traditional craft while meeting contemporary luxury expectations. The defining insight: heritage branding fails when it becomes costume. Instead of surface-level cultural motifs, I studied mandala geometry's structural principles and wove them into the grid system, typography spacing, and illustration compositions. The identity feels rooted without being nostalgic, giving the hotel a visual voice as layered as its context.

09 TEDxBUV Website, Social Designs 2023
8:3 hero
Logo

Designed the event website and social media visual system for TEDxBUV, the independently organized TED conference at our university. My role covered digital experience design and campaign visuals across multiple platforms. The central insight: TEDx events often default to borrowing TED's visual authority rather than building their own voice. I designed a system respecting TED's guidelines while carving a distinct identity — bold typographic treatments, a dynamic color system tied to speaker themes, and social templates flexible enough for the volunteer team to maintain consistency without a designer present at every step.
