Background

I'm Thành Lâm — a multidisciplinary designer and marketer based in Vietnam, trained in Marketing at British University Vietnam. I build brands from the name up: identity, print, packaging, interface, and the campaign that carries them. One mind across the whole stack means nothing gets lost in the handoff.

I came up through marketing, so I never design in a vacuum — every mark, layout, and frame is built to move a real audience. I've shaped identities across environmental tech, beauty, hospitality, and student culture, and I sweat the details most people never notice. That's usually where the work earns its keep.

Philosophy

Design and marketing aren't two jobs to me — they're one. A logo that doesn't sell is decoration; a campaign without craft is noise. I build the whole world a brand lives in, then make sure it actually performs.

My range is the point: branding, print, UI/UX, photography, film, illustration. When I'm not designing I'm behind a camera or in the edit — chasing the kind of detail that turns a nice idea into one people remember. I'm early in the career, fast on the tools, and never satisfied with "good enough."
